How to make simple tacos

To make simple tacos you will need 5 easy ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Meat(asada)
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa
  • 1. Having the *comal heated up throw in lets say two tortillas.
  • 2. Place your meat in a pan/skillet(while stirring every 30 seconds) and let it cook till the color changes to what you see im the pictures above(slight well done)
  • 3. With the tortillas nice and hot in a plate, place a generous ammount of the cooked meat on op of the tortillas.
  • 4. Cut an onion into tiny squares as well as cutting the long stems from the cilantro.
  • 5. Place the veggies on top of the meat.
  • 6. Now add a good ammount of your favorite salsa on the tacos just careful not to drown them.
  • 7. Now enjoy!
